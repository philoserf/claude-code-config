# Output Templates

## Full Reflection Format

Matches the Step 3 recap structure in SKILL.md.

```markdown
---
created: YYYY-MM-DD
---

## What we worked on

Goal and final state.

## Decisions & rationale

Choices made and why.

## Surprises & dead ends

What didn't work or wasn't expected.

## Open threads

Anything left unfinished or worth revisiting.

## Suggested CLAUDE.md updates

Guidance that belongs in a tracked instructions file rather than auto-memory (omit if none).
```

## Compact Format

For shorter sessions, use this condensed format:

```markdown
---
created: YYYY-MM-DD
---

## Quick Reflection: [Date]

**What we did**: [Brief summary]

**Key learnings**:

- [Learning 1]
- [Learning 2]

**For CLAUDE.md**:

- [Update 1]
- [Update 2]

**Next time**:

- [Improvement 1]
```

## Storage

Reflections are saved to Obsidian. See the "Save to Obsidian" step in SKILL.md for the canonical storage approach.
