The three-line motto is now:

- HOMO·HOMINIS·ESSE (ontological ground)
- COGITA·DISCE·NECTE·ENUNTIA (epistemic/formation cycle)
- PERCIPE·STRUE·EFFICE (pragmatic/execution cycle)

CONNECTE was replaced with STRUE (build/construct) because NECTE in the formation cycle already covers connection/weaving, making CONNECTE redundant. STRUE adds a distinct concept.

**Why:** Mark's decision after discussion about redundancy between NECTE and CONNECTE across the two cycles.

**How to apply:** Use STRUE not CONNECTE in any new content. Note: the draft essay "A Layered Anthropology Ground, Knowing, Doing" in the Obsidian vault still has explanatory prose describing CONNECTE's meaning — that needs manual revision to reflect STRUE's semantics (build/construct vs. connect/relate).
